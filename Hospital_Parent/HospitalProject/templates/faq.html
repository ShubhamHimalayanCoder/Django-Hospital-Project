Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What are your visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 9 AM to 5 PM. However, exceptions may be made for special circumstances. Please check with our staff for more information.

2. How do I schedule an appointment?

To schedule an appointment, you can call our appointment line at +91-7977879879 or use our online appointment booking system on our website.

3. What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance information, any relevant medical records or test results, a list of current medications, and a form of identification.

4. Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we do accept walk-in patients. However, we recommend calling ahead or using our online appointment booking system whenever possible to minimize wait times.

5. What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please check with your insurance provider or contact our billing department for specific information about accepted insurance plans.

6. How can I pay my bill?

You can pay your bill online through our secure payment portal, by mail, or in person at our billing office. We accept various forms of payment, including credit/debit cards, cheques, UPI and cash.

7. Do you offer financial assistance or payment plans?

Yes, we understand that medical expenses can be overwhelming. We offer financial assistance programs and flexible payment plans to eligible patients. Please contact our billing department for more information.

8. How do I request medical records?

To request your medical records, please fill out the appropriate form available on our website and submit it to our medical records department. You may also contact our medical records department for assistance.

9. What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency, dial 112 immediately or proceed to the nearest emergency room. If you are unsure whether your situation constitutes an emergency, you can also call our hospital's emergency line for guidance.

10. How can I provide feedback or file a complaint?

We welcome feedback from our patients and strive to continuously improve our services. You can provide feedback or file a complaint by contacting our patient relations department via phone or email or through our online portal. Your input is valuable to us.
